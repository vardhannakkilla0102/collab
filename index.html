Indian Tourism Dashboard

Explore top travel destinations in India

Goa

Goa, located on the western coast of India, is famous for its stunning beaches, vibrant nightlife, and Portuguese heritage. Popular spots include Baga Beach, Fort Aguada, and the Basilica of Bom Jesus.

Kerala

Known as "God's Own Country", Kerala is renowned for its backwaters, Ayurveda treatments, hill stations like Munnar, and cultural traditions such as Kathakali dance.

Rajasthan

Rajasthan, the land of kings, is known for its deserts, palaces, and forts. Jaipur, Udaipur, Jodhpur, and Jaisalmer are key cities showcasing rich Rajput architecture and culture.

Kashmir

Often called "Paradise on Earth", Kashmir offers breathtaking views of snow-capped mountains, Dal Lake shikara rides, and lush valleys. Gulmarg and Pahalgam are must-visit spots.

Delhi

Delhi, the capital city of India, is a blend of ancient history and modern culture. Visit landmarks like India Gate, Red Fort, Qutub Minar, and experience vibrant markets and street food.
